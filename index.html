Welcome to the Federation

We are a block-level federation working to empower communities in Peddamandyam through inclusive development, common land restoration, and sustainable livelihoods.

About the Federation

Peddamandyam Mandal, now part of Annamayya district, comprises 17 Gram Panchayats and 153 habitations. It is a backward and drought-prone area where agriculture and livestock are main livelihoods. The area has poor access to education and health and struggles with soil erosion, overuse of chemical inputs, and groundwater depletion. The federation is formed to strengthen village institutions, secure tenure over commons, and build resilient livelihoods through collective governance.
